The clinic and the codebase have more in common than most people think.

Both require precision. Both demand a mental model of a system you cannot fully see. And both are, at their core, about solving problems for people who trust you with something they care about.

This is an exploration of how I think about dentistry through the lens of a builder — and technology through the lens of a clinician.
